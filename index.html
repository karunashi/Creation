Creation begins, but alas, it's a clean slate with nothing to resemble what a planet should look like. The world merely remains as an empty canvas, to be filled with whatever it requires to be complete and whole.

It's up to you, Creator, to bring life and fill the empty world with the basic four elements. The four elements are presented to you at the center of your sight. Each element will bequeath random numerical values to the world's completion. Lastly, direct your attention to "Destiny," which should indicate how much of the total elements in numerical value is required.
